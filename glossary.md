# 深度学习术语表（中英文对照）

本术语表整理了《Little Book of Deep Learning》书中出现的关键深度学习术语，提供中英文对照以便参考。

## 基础概念

- **层** (layers)
- **线性层** (linear layers)
- **卷积层** (convolutional layers)
- **注意力层** (attention layers)
- **池化** (pooling)
- **Dropout** (dropout) - 随机丢弃
- **批归一化** (batch normalization)
- **层归一化** (layer normalization)
- **残差连接** (residual connections)
- **跳跃连接** (skip connections) - 更一般的连接方式
- **令牌嵌入** (token embeddings)
- **位置编码** (positional encoding)

## 模型架构

- **多层感知机** (multilayer perceptron, MLP)
- **卷积神经网络** (convolutional neural network, CNN)
- **循环神经网络** (recurrent neural network, RNN)
- **长短期记忆网络** (long short-term memory, LSTM)
- **门控循环单元** (gated recurrent unit, GRU)
- **Transformer** - 基于注意力的架构
- **Vision Transformer (ViT)** - 视觉Transformer
- **生成对抗网络** (generative adversarial network, GAN)
- **变分自编码器** (variational autoencoder, VAE)
- **图神经网络** (graph neural network, GNN)
- **残差网络** (residual network, ResNet)
- **编码器-解码器架构** (encoder-decoder architecture)

## 训练相关

- **损失函数** (loss function)
- **交叉熵损失** (cross-entropy loss)
- **均方误差** (mean squared error, MSE)
- **梯度下降** (gradient descent)
- **随机梯度下降** (stochastic gradient descent, SGD)
- **反向传播** (backpropagation)
- **梯度消失** (vanishing gradient)
- **梯度爆炸** (exploding gradient)
- **优化器** (optimizer)
- **Adam优化器** (Adam optimizer)
- **学习率** (learning rate)
- **批次大小** (batch size)
- **训练集/验证集/测试集** (training/validation/test set)
- **过拟合** (overfitting)
- **欠拟合** (underfitting)
- **正则化** (regularization)
- **数据增强** (data augmentation)

## 应用技术

- **自监督学习** (self-supervised learning)
- **少样本学习** (few-shot learning)
- **零样本学习** (zero-shot learning)
- **提示工程** (prompt engineering)
- **思维链** (chain-of-thought)
- **检索增强生成** (retrieval-augmented generation, RAG)
- **量化** (quantization)
- **低秩适应** (low-rank adaptation, LoRA)
- **量化低秩适应** (quantized low-rank adaptation, QLoRA)
- **模型合并** (model merging)
- **对齐调整** (alignment tuning)
- **从人类反馈中强化学习** (reinforcement learning from human feedback, RLHF)

## 著名模型

- **GPT** (Generative Pre-trained Transformer)
- **CLIP** (Contrastive Language-Image Pre-training)
- **BERT** (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers)
- **ViT** (Vision Transformer)
- **ResNet** (Residual Network)
- **U-Net** (U-shaped Network)
- **YOLO** (You Only Look Once)
- **SSD** (Single Shot Detector)
- **DQN** (Deep Q-Network)

## 硬件与计算

- **GPU** (Graphics Processing Unit)
- **TPU** (Tensor Processing Unit)
- **张量** (tensor)
- **批次处理** (batching)
- **并行计算** (parallel computing)
- **内存占用** (memory footprint)
- **推理** (inference)
- **训练** (training)

## 评估指标

- **准确率** (accuracy)
- **精确率** (precision)
- **召回率** (recall)
- **F1分数** (F1 score)
- **均方误差** (mean squared error, MSE)
- **平均绝对误差** (mean absolute error, MAE)

## 注意事项

1. 本术语表基于《Little Book of Deep Learning》书中出现的术语整理
2. 部分术语保留了英文原名（如Transformer、GPT、CLIP），这些在中文技术文献中常用英文表示
3. 术语翻译力求准确，但不同文献可能有所差异
4. 对于有多个中文译法的术语，选择了本书中使用的主要译法
